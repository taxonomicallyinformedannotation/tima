Usage: 
  fake_features_edges.R [--fil-fea-raw=<fil-fea-raw>] [--fil-net-spe-edg-pro=<fil-net-spe-edg-pro>] [--names-features=<names-features>]

Arguments:
  fil-fea-raw                Raw features file. STRING
