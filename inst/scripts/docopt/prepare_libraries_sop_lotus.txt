  Rscript inst/scripts/prepare_libraries_sop_lotus.R --fil-lib-sop-raw-ecm data/source/libraries/sop/lotus.csv.gz --fil-lib-sop-pro data/interim/libraries/sop/lotus_prepared.tsv.gz

  prepare_libraries_sop_ecmdb.R [--fil-lib-sop-raw-ecm=<fil-lib-sop-raw-ecm>] [--fil-lib-sop-pro=<fil-lib-sop-pro>]

Arguments:
  fil-lib-sop-raw-lot        LOTUS raw structure organism pairs library. STRING
